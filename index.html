About Us

Wheelers Wood-Craft is a family run business whom use recycled wood to make fantastic rustic furniture. The business was established in 2010 when the first EUR pallets was turned into a TV stand. From this the business has expanded over the years to include recycled sleepers.
From when I was a child I was always messing about with wood, making treehouses, ladders and furniture for the treehouse, which you could back in the early 90s. This all started from a Meccano set, from there my dad got me into helping him build a wooden desk for his home and fixing sheds and fences. All the skills I have learnt have been self-taught, from books and now the Internet. I use to use new timber but found this to be costly for the build. By using recycled wood the cost is more than half for the materials, and this bring prices right down, this is also helping to keeping green and saving the forests.
When required new wood is used for some custom builds i.e. wooden vivarium, kids low profile bed, kitchen sliding cupboard, workshop desks, router table, bike shed, walk in wardrobes and many other varied items.
